Aska Adventure Area

Aska Adventure Area, near Blue Ridge, GA, is a popular outdoor recreation destination known for its scenic hiking and mountain biking trails. The area spans 17 miles of trails that wind through the Chattahoochee National Forest, offering breathtaking views of mountains, rivers, and forests. It is also a hub for fishing, tubing, and other water-based activities along the Toccoa River.

About Blue Ridge

Blue Ridge, GA, is a charming mountain town known for its stunning natural beauty, with access to the Appalachian Mountains and the scenic Blue Ridge Lake. The town offers a mix of outdoor activities, from hiking and fishing to scenic railway tours, along with a vibrant arts scene, boutique shopping, and farm-to-table dining. Its peaceful atmosphere and breathtaking views make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and those seeking a relaxing mountain retreat.
